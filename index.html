DealerEdge ensures your dealership dominates visibility across search engines, third-party marketplaces, and digital ads. AI-powered SEO/AIO content, optimized listings, and high-performing ad creative work together to attract high-intent buyers before they ever reach a competitor.

Brand Assets

  • SEO-driven blog articles and landing pages
  • AI-generated ad creatives (display, social, video snippets)
  • High-ranking third-party listings (BoatTrader, RVTrader, etc.)
  • Keyword-optimized product pages
  • Brand-consistent imagery (studio-quality product visuals)
  • Short-form social content (reels, posts, teaser clips)
Customer Journey · Consideration

Engage Every Shopper Instantly

Once a customer lands on your site or submits an inquiry, speed and clarity are everything. DealerEdge activates real-time engagement across chat, text, email, and phone, answering questions, surfacing inventory, and guiding users toward the right product. Every interaction is intelligent, personalized, and designed to keep momentum moving forward.

Brand Assets

  • High-conversion website UI/UX (fast, modern, OEM-grade design)

  • Automated email/text response templates (personalized messaging)
  • Interactive inventory pages with filters and saved favorites
  • FAQ libraries generated from real customer questions
  • Retargeting ads showing recently viewed products
Customer Journey · Purchase

Turn Interest Into Action

At the decision stage, DealerEdge removes friction and accelerates conversion. AI qualifies leads, schedules appointments, and delivers rich customer insights to your sales team, so every interaction is informed and intentional. The result is a faster, smoother path from inquiry to signed deal.

Brand Assets

  • Lead capture forms optimized for conversion
  • Appointment scheduling interfaces (test drives, demos)
  • CRM-integrated lead profiles with behavioral data
  • Personalized follow-up messaging (email/text sequences)
  • Sales enablement dashboards for reps
  • Conversion-focused landing pages (offers, promotions, incentives)

Stay Connected After the Sale

The relationship doesn't end at purchase, it evolves. DealerEdge continues engagement through automated follow-ups, service reminders, and relevant content that adds value. Customers feel supported, informed, and connected to your dealership long after they leave the lot.

Brand Assets

  • Automated email nurture campaigns (service reminders, tips, upgrades)
  • Post-purchase onboarding content (videos, guides, FAQs)
  • Service scheduling interfaces
  • Personalized check-in messages from "AI Sales Captain"
  • Educational blog content and ownership resources
  • Social engagement (community posts, feature highlights)
